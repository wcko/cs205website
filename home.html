Machine Learning:
A Predictive Traffic Model

We use parallel processing computational techniques to analyze the "big data" of 1 billion geo-referenced observations of traffic recorded by the thousands of traffic detectors in downtown Los Angeles over long time frames. Using the message passing interface (MPI) to distribute processing tasks, we've created an efficient and robust software package to model and predict the the traffic in Los Angeles for any given time period. With real-time traffic updates, the model continually "learns" and regenerates based on a real-time data stream.

Explore our site to find traffic in real time, or to see a prediction we've made.
